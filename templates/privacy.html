Privacy
Policy

Your financial data is personal. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using Spendly.

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic profile information such as your name and email address. As you use the service, we store the expense records you enter — including amount, category, date, and description. We also collect minimal technical data such as your browser type and session activity to keep the service secure and functional.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly — to display your transactions, generate category breakdowns and monthly summaries, and let you filter by time period. We do not use your financial records for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers and protected with industry-standard safeguards. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You may request export or deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We retain data only for as long as your account is active or as required to comply with applicable laws.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, and error monitoring. These providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy commitments. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, want to exercise your data rights, or wish to report a privacy concern, please contact us through the support channel listed on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
